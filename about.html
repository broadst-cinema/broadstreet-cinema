ABOUT US

Broadstreet Cinema was founded in late 2019 by a group of friends and college students studying at SUNY Plattsburgh. Since then, we've made a total of two films, with several more on the way, and we've worked with many successful clients.

Our original team of five has since grown to include over 10,000 employees. And you could be one of them! Looking to join our crew? Fill out an application today!

Interested in our services? We're always looking for new projects to take on! Contact us today for a quote.
